The 20-Min Daily Drill

  • 01.

    The Stationary Orbit (5m)

    Keep a single object in the center of your screen while flying a perfect circle around it.

  • 02.

    The Figure 8 (5m)

    Coordinate yaw and roll to maintain smooth, consistent turns through two markers.

  • 03.

    Throttle Management (10m)

    Fly under low obstacles (like tables or pipes) and pop up instantly to a hover.
